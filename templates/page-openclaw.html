Common Questions

Do I need my own server?

You can run OpenClaw on a Mac Mini, a VPS, or most Linux machines. We'll recommend the best option based on your situation during the discovery call.

Which AI model does it use?

OpenClaw works with Claude, GPT, DeepSeek, and others. We'll help you pick the right one based on your needs and budget.

Is this safe?

When configured properly — yes. That's the whole point of hiring someone who knows what they're doing. We follow security best practices including sandbox mode, restricted permissions, and vetted skill installations.

What if I need changes later?

We offer follow-up support packages if you want to add new skills, connect additional channels, or adjust your setup down the road.

Can it really replace a virtual assistant?

For many routine tasks — email triage, scheduling, reminders, research, message handling — yes. It won't replace human judgment for complex decisions, but it handles the repetitive stuff so you can focus on what matters.
